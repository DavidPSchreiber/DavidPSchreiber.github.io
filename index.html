My Story

Act I: after graduating from NYU Film School, I worked extensively in the lighting, editing and story departments of various motion picture companies.
Act II: higher education. Going from instructor to director to dean, my most enduring lesson was that there are no teachers, only learners--and the best instructors intuitively embrace the belief.
Act III: while working as a dean at an art college, I fell in love with web design and development. As someone both left- and right-brained, I feel a thrill of pride when I'm able to harmonize function and form to tell a compelling story about person, product or service. I hope to tell your story.
